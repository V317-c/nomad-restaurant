Welcome To
Nomad Restaurant & Bar

Nestled in the heart of Junnar, Nomad Restaurant and Bar is a haven for food enthusiasts seeking a true taste of India. Our story is one of passion, where we aim to share the vibrant tapestry of Indian cuisine and the warmth of Indian hospitality.

We invite you to embark on a sensory journey like no other, where each dish carries a tale and every meal is an exploration of authentic flavors. At Nomad, we don't just celebrate the culinary arts; our fully-stocked bar, offering an array of exotic drinks and mocktails, ensures that every visit is a complete experience. It's the perfect spot for chilling out with friends and family, where the laughter is contagious, and the memories are unforgettable. Come to Nomad Restaurant and Bar, and let your taste buds dance with delight and your spirit soar in the heart of India's culinary charm.

Where Every Bite Tells A Tale

Our restaurant boasts an inviting and cozy atmosphere where you can savor the finest Indian, Mughlai, and Chinese delicacies. Every dish tells a story of the diverse cultures that make up the heart of our menu. From the fiery spices of North India to the delicate flavors of the Mughlai cuisine and the exotic allure of Chinese dishes, each bite is an adventure in itself.

Crafting Culinary Magic Daily

Our commitment to culinary excellence is unwavering. At Nomad, we take pride in the art of blending spices, marinating meats, and balancing flavors to perfection. Our Indian specialties like Butter Chicken and Naan will transport you to the streets of Delhi. The Mughlai offering, with its succulent kebabs and aromatic biryanis, showcases the regal essence of Indian cuisine.

OUR SPECIALITIES
What We Master At
" Indian

At Nomad Restaurant and Bar, we take immense pride in the rich tapestry of Indian cuisine. Indian food is a symphony of flavors, a dance of spices, and a celebration of diversity. Our chefs masterfully prepare classics like Butter Chicken, where tender, succulent chicken is enveloped in a luscious tomato-based gravy, and Rogan Josh, a slow-cooked lamb curry that embodies the essence of Kashmiri cuisine. Each bite of our Indian specialties tells the story of a nation where food is not just sustenance; it's a cultural experience.

" Mughlai

Our Mughlai dishes are a royal feast for the senses. The Mughals, known for their opulent lifestyle and exquisite culinary traditions, inspire our menu. Savory kebabs, biryanis brimming with saffron and rich spices, and creamy Shahi Paneer are just a few examples of our Mughlai delights. Dining at Nomad is like stepping into a Mughal palace, where each dish is a masterpiece that reflects the grandeur and luxury of that era.

" Chinese

In our quest to offer a unique culinary experience, we've incorporated the enchanting flavors of Chinese cuisine. Our Chinese menu takes you on a journey through the vibrant streets of China. The Szechuan Chicken, a fiery stir-fry, is a favorite among those who crave bold and spicy flavors. Whether you're savoring our sweet and sour dishes or indulging in the comforting embrace of fried rice, our Chinese specialties harmoniously blend Eastern and Western influences, creating a fusion of taste that's truly extraordinary.
